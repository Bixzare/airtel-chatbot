Zamani Niger Services and Information
Mobile Services:
Zamani Niger (formerly Orange Niger) provides full mobile telecommunications services including voice calls, SMS, mobile data, and Zamani Cash mobile money. Their network includes 2G, 3G, and 4G+ coverage across Niger, with 100% coverage in Niamey and strong presence in all major towns and cities.
